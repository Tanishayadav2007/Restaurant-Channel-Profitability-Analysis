# 🍽️ Restaurant Channel Profitability Analysis

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes the profitability of multiple restaurant sales channels, including:

- In-Store
- Uber Eats
- DoorDash
- Self-Delivery

The goal is to determine which channels generate sustainable profits rather than simply increasing revenue.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Compare revenue and net profit across sales channels
- Measure profit margin for each channel
- Quantify commission drag from third-party platforms
- Evaluate Self-Delivery ROI
- Identify the most profitable cuisine and restaurant segment
- Analyze financial risk using Profit Volatility
- Generate business recommendations through data-driven insights

# 📈 Major Insights

✅ In-Store generated the highest profit margin.

✅ Self-Delivery achieved significantly higher profitability than third-party platforms.

✅ Uber Eats generated the highest revenue but experienced substantial commission drag.

✅ Higher revenue does not necessarily result in higher profitability.

✅ Operating expenses and commissions are the major contributors to margin erosion.
